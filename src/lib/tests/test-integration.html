EmailGenius - ConvertKIT Integration Test

Test Instructions

  1. Open http://localhost:3010 in another tab
  2. Enter a financial product URL (e.g., https://us.topfinanzas.com/financial-solutions/capital-one-quicksilver-student-credit-card-requirements-application)
  3. Click "Generate Email Campaign"
  4. Wait for the AI to generate the content
  5. Click any of the individual copy buttons
  6. Verify the notification shows ConvertKIT guidance
  7. Paste the content into a text editor to verify Markdown formatting is preserved

Expected Behavior

  • Copy buttons should have blue styling (not gray)
  • Notification should mention ConvertKIT and "Yes, format" guidance
  • Copied content should preserve **bold text** and line breaks
  • Email body should maintain proper paragraph structure
  • Department signatures should be properly formatted with **bold**
